about US

At BMW, driving is more than just transportation—it’s an experience built on passion, innovation, and precision. Since 1916, Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) has been at the forefront of engineering excellence, combining performance with luxury to create vehicles that inspire confidence on every road. From our pioneering beginnings as an aircraft engine manufacturer to becoming one of the world’s most respected automobile and motorcycle brands, BMW has always stayed true to its mission: delivering Sheer Driving Pleasure.

Our Philosophy

Every BMW is crafted with a perfect balance of power, agility, and design. Whether it’s the thrill of the M Series, the elegance of the 7 Series, or the future-forward innovation of our electric BMW i models, we strive to make every journey unforgettable.

Innovation & Sustainability

BMW leads the industry in sustainable mobility, blending cutting-edge technology with eco-friendly initiatives. Through electric and hybrid innovations, we’re shaping the future of driving while staying committed to reducing our environmental footprint.
